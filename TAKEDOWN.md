# Rights Concerns

To report a copyright, trademark, or other rights concern, email
copyright@fluxer.app.

Please include:

- The affected file or data entry.
- The right you believe is affected.
- Your relationship to the rights holder.
- The change you are requesting.
- A reliable way to contact you.

Maintainers should review rights concerns promptly, remove or revise disputed
material when appropriate, and keep the reporter informed of the resolution.
